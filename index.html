This video illustrates the fundamental idea behind rCFD: comparing a reference frame (time step) with all other frames by quantifying how mass (solid fraction) shifts from one cell to another. The degree of similarity between frames is based on the time required for these cell-to-cell shifts, enabling the construction of a recurrence matrix for predictive modeling.
